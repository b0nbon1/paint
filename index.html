Our History

Officially started and registered in 2018, GDP LTD COMPANY is owned by George Odhiambo Ochieng who has master the art of painting for a period of over 20 years. The company takes painting contracts and sub contracts from clients which includes paint production companies in Kenya. Paint work is vast field which entails a lot of services. GDP has a team of skilled workers who are experts in most of the fields and never disappoint. For the short period we have done a number of projects in the construction field, new projects and renovations. GDP services includes wall paintings, wall coatings, special effects on wall, road markings, gypsum fixing and design, epoxy application, wood finishes e.t.c. In GDP clients satisfaction is our concern as quality is our priority.

our services

Commercial Painting

When you want to make a lasting impression in your showroom, office, or store front, then check out all the options available to you through The Geoderricks Painters.. We can provide a wide array of tones, colors, and designs that give any your room in your building that wow factor!

Exterior Painting

If you want a long lasting, durable paint job that gives your home or office a distinct look, then hire us for your new paint job.

we can bring new life to your property’s exterior by protecting your home with exterior coats that retain their vibrancy for years to come.

Interior Painting

Revive that home or office interior with a fresh coat of paint.. We can help you create a complete unique feel for your room with innovative designs and a broad spectrum of colors to suite your taste. Whether you are painting your room for the first time or need to revamp that old faded color, contact us.

Painting Consultant

Sit down with any of our industry experts about any number of painting topics. Want to give that old room a new look, but have no idea where to begin? We offer consulting that is designed to help you create a vision that works with your personal taste and that matches your entire room from top to bottom.

Wallpaper Removal

Most home owners do not have the experience or knowledge to remove wallpaper in a way that is thorough and doesn’t damage the wall.

We provide efficient, messy-free wallpaper removal that saves you considerable time and frustration.

Gel Stain Fiberglass Doors

Gel Staining is a highly specialized detailed job that requires absolute care and precision. A fresh gel stain will give your door a totally brand new look sure to impress! Gel staining can be done for both exterior and interior doors in both residential and commercial properties.
